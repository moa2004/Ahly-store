About Us - Al-Ahly Egyptian Club

Welcome to the club of the century!

Al-Ahly Sporting Club, commonly referred to as Al-Ahly, is an Egyptian professional sports club based in Cairo. Founded in 1907, Al-Ahly is one of the oldest and most successful football clubs in Africa and the Middle East. Throughout its illustrious history, the club has garnered a massive following and has achieved remarkable success both domestically and internationally.

Al-Ahly's dominance in Egyptian football is unparalleled. The club has won the Egyptian Premier League title more than 40 times, making it the most successful club in the country's football history. Al-Ahly's relentless pursuit of excellence has seen it maintain a consistent level of performance, setting the benchmark for success in Egyptian football.

However, Al-Ahly's success extends beyond the borders of Egypt. The club has established itself as a force to be reckoned with on the continental stage, with numerous triumphs in the CAF Champions League, Africa's premier club competition. Al-Ahly's victories in the Champions League have solidified its status as one of the most dominant clubs in African football history.

Al-Ahly's success is not limited to football. The club has also enjoyed success in other sports such as basketball, handball, and volleyball. Its basketball team, for example, has won numerous Egyptian Basketball League titles and has competed in international competitions. Al-Ahly's achievements across multiple sports demonstrate its commitment to excellence and its status as a multidimensional sporting institution.

What truly sets Al-Ahly apart is its passionate fan base. The club's supporters, known as the "Ultras Ahlawy," are renowned for their unwavering loyalty and fervent support. Whether at home or abroad, the Ultras Ahlawy can always be counted on to create an electrifying atmosphere and inspire the players to perform at their best. Their unwavering dedication to the club is a testament to the deep-rooted connection between Al-Ahly and its fans.

As Al-Ahly continues to strive for excellence, its legacy as the club of the century remains undisputed. With a rich history of success and a bright future ahead, Al-Ahly Egyptian Club will continue to inspire generations of athletes and fans alike. Whether on the football pitch or in the hearts of its supporters, Al-Ahly will always be synonymous with greatness.
